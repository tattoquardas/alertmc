KOAN TRAINING


The Koan-PI questionnaire is based on extensive empirical research. It has proven an accurate instrument for predicting personality traits, attitudes and behaviour.

At the end of the four-day certification training, participants are able to
a) Understand Koan-PI’s specific qualities as a psychometric instrument;
b) Interpret the personality profile and other information generated by Koan-PI and effectively relate these insights to the subject’s job requirements;
c) Offer the candidates or participants feedback with regard to selection, appointments or career development;
d) Integrate Koan-PI within their organisation and use the Koan-PI instruments to create added value.
