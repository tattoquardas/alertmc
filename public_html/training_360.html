360° FEEDBACK TRAINING

The Alert360° platform offers you a broad range of questionnaires – plus the corresponding reports. These items can be used in the context of a professional development programme. The 360° feedback cycle is a structured method for gaining relevant feedback from people in the subject’s professional environment, on the basis of concrete behaviour and behavioural indicators. Provided you select the most suitable questionnaire – or compile one that is tailored to your requirements – and optimally support the process with feedback and the elaboration of a Personal Development Plan (PDP), the 360° feedback cycle can be a highly educational experience with a lasting impact.

If you would like to be fully prepared before starting up the 360° feedback cycle and feedback programme, you can contact us for a dedicated two-day training. During this training, you will learn how to make effective use of the Alert360° platform and discover how to use Alert360° as key element in a successful leadership development programme or a programme focussed on other team members or target groups.
