ASSESMENT CENTRES
Recruiting potential.

The Assessment Centre tool allows us to make standardised measurements of an individual’s various levels of competence.

These measurements can serve as the base for both a strength/weakness analysis and a broader picture of the candidate’s general potential.

The Assessment Centre tool will consistently yield a carefully considered and well-substantiated YES-NO assessment.


Alert Management Consultants offers organisations a helping hand in the assessment and evaluation of candidates from both beyond and within the organisation in the context of:
- The recruitment of new candidates
- The promotion of an employee to a new position in the short term
- The identification of high-potential candidates who can be appointed to complex positions of responsibility, or in the context of a management development programme.


Will you be commissioning an assessment as the employer?
1. Preparatory interview

To optimally tailor the assessment process to your wishes, we will first organise a preparatory interview. During this interview, we make a clear inventory of your wishes, requirements and expectations. We can either take your organisation’s existing competence model as a point of departure. Or we can develop a custom set of Assessment Centre exercises that are aligned as closely as possible to the actual demands of the position and your organisation. During this interview, we will also confer with you to determine the best reporting format for the assessment results.


2. Assessment

An average Assessment Centre session lasts 5 to 6 hours and consists of the following elements:
- Facet Intelligence Test: Provides insight in the candidate’s capacity to solve numerical and verbal challenges.
- In-basket test with roleplaying: Provides insight in the candidate’s handling of complex situations, how he or she takes decisions and sets priorities, and whether the candidate has the potential to effectively handle roles specific to the position.
- Interview: Provides insight in the extent to which the candidate has already demonstrated particular competences in the past. In addition, this interview deals in greater depth with the candidate’s expectations and ambitions.
- Feedback: At the end of the session, we provide the candidate with extensive personalised feedback.


Before participating in the Assessment Centre, candidates are asked to complete the KOAN-PI personality test. This test provides insight into how likely it is that the candidate will demonstrate specific competences in future roles. The candidate will be provided with a descriptive report that goes into the findings of the KOAN-PI Personality Test.


3. Communication of results

We will communicate the results of all Assessment Centre elements to you by means of a tailored report. In addition, we will extensively discuss our findings, recommendations and possible integration suggestions with you.
