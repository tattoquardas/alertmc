ABOUT US
Alert Management Consultants (AMC), established in 1992 by Lieven Cornelis, Alert Management Consultants (AMC) has been active in a variety of HRM domains from the very outset. Innovation and research are in our genes, and our motto has always been

'Why choose simplicity if you can tackle complexity'.


In the mid-1990s, AMC started performing targeted, empirical research to gain input for the development of various proprietary personality tests, and specifically the Koan-PI personality test. Over the years, we have added a number of other HRM instruments, including ALTI, which yields a reliable measurement of 16 Psychological Types; Alert360°, an extensive platform with 360° feedback questionnaires and the associated reports; PolyGlot language tests, the Facet capacities test and a collection of In- basket Exercises. Our clients use these instruments – to their considerable satisfaction – for a variety of purposes, including selection, personal development, leadership development and team building. Our questionnaires and reports can be accessed online. As far as training is concerned, we offer a wide range of in-house programmes, courses and workshops on all sorts of themes, including team building, communication, performance management and behavioural interviews. A number of our programmes use MBTI theory or Psychological Types as a point of departure. And finally, we offer accreditation trainings for the users of our various HRM instruments.


In terms of HRM advisory services, AMC can offer your organisation concrete support and recommendations in the areas of coaching, assessment & development centres, 360° feedback and career counselling. In each case, you have the choice of two options: we can either teach you how to handle the implementation and management process in-house, or you can ask us to handle it on your behalf.


In addition, you can take advantage of our extensive competence management experience. We can help you to effectively incorporate competences and talent development within your own HRM programmes. But you can also turn to us to set up a tailored competence management project for your organisation.
