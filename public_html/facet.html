FACET

Our intelligence test, called Facet, is based on the idea of intelligence consisting of different facets or factors. These three global factors are verbal, numerical and visual-special skills. These three factors together give an indication of someone's general intellectual capacities.

o Verbal skills
o Numerical skills
o Visuo-spatial skills


Facet also goes beyond the traditional intelligence test. You have the option of testing two extra facets, as follows:

• Memory measurement. In this test the person being tested has to read texts and look at pictures for a specified length of time. Afterwards, the person responds to questions which indicate how much he/she is still able to remember.
• Speed and accuracy measurement, where the candidate performs a task requiring attention.
