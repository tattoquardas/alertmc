ALTI TRAINING

The Alert Type Indicator (ALTI) is a psychological instrument that helps you increase your insight into your own psychological make-up and that of others. It allows you to discover your personal strengths and unique talents. The instrument also deals with domains that you may want to develop further. ALTI can make a significant contribution to your personal development and stimulates growth opportunities in the very broadest sense of the word. An interesting side-effect of this increased self-knowledge is that you find it easier to understand what drives those around you. You are able to empathise with behaviour that previously annoyed you, for example, leading to more productive collaborations.

Allow us to offer an example:

Imagine you like to prepare everything well in advance. You draw up a clear agenda for an upcoming meeting and make sure it is distributed to everyone who will be participating. If people start deviating from this agenda during the meeting in question, you may find this annoying. In contrast, others may actually be irritated by strict adherence to the agenda items. It is impossible, in other words, to organise a meeting that it is perfect for everyone involved. Instead of becoming annoyed by this ‘mismatch’, you can also use it to your benefit. The type theory can help you in this process.
