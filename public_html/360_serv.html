360° FEEDBACK

As a manager, one of the most valuable things you can receive is targeted feedback. And when it comes from a variety of sources and is generated by a carefully-considered questionnaire, it becomes even more valuable as input for your professional growth. Over the past few decades, the 360° feedback cycle has established itself as a well-known method for gaining concrete, systematic feedback from your immediate professional environment.

You can turn to AMC for a comprehensive range of services in this domain.

360° feedback projects:

Since our establishment, we have regularly handled 360° feedback projects on behalf of our clients. These projects include one or more of the following elements:
✔ Joint selection of the most suitable questionnaires ;
✔ Information session for the participants;
✔ Logistics services and support;
✔ Preparation of the reports;
✔ Provision of feedback to the feedback subject;
✔ Guidance and support of the subject in the elaboration of a Plan of Action and/or Personal Development Plan (PDP);
✔ Consultation with the subject and his/her direct manager & HRM regarding the transfer of ownership of the PDP to the subject and his/her organisation.

We can be relied on to provide support services for both individual management-level employees and larger groups.
