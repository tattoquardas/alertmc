HR TOOLS

Alert MC focuses on the development and provision of effective HRM instruments. People’s personality and inherent strengths and points of attention, Psychological Type, individual preferences and their impact on personal development and interaction with others, testing self-knowledge against feedback from one’s environment: these are all extremely interesting domains. And we have elaborated specialised instruments for each of them, including Koan-PI, with the associated Professional Balance, Sales DNA and Project Management reports; the Alert Type Indicator (ALTI); and an extensive platform of 360° feedback facilities. In the process, we are happy to share our expertise with consultants and internal HRM professionals via a wide range of reports, as well as programmes that train them in the use of the aforementioned instruments.
